The front-end

DRIS workflows

  • About
  • Collection: You could think of a collection as a labelled box; it’s filled with various items and groupings of items that are all ‘children’ ( series and Items) of that box stored in the warehouse alongside many other boxes.
  • Series: This record would be another basic record presenting a basic overview of a grouping of items located in a box (collection).( few notebooks, a stack of photographs, some letters and/or objects such as a typewriter or pipe)
  • Item: item is anything with a distinct title (book, poster, photograph, manuscript, etc), which may or may not have multiple surrogates connected underneath it.
